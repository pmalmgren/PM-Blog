About me

Mon, Sep 24, 2018

I’m a dad and software engineer. I live in the mountains of North Carolina, and currently work as a Product Engineer at Zapier.

I’ve worked for a few other organizations, but the work I’m most proud of is my time as an electrician in the Navy, and my research for Dr. Souvenir in Computer Vision and Machine Learing.

When I’m not parenting or working I enjoy hiking, reading, podcasts, learning, lifting weights, cooking, and occasionally writing on this blog.
